Volunteering in Madagascar

VIM

Give your time. Change the world. Discover an island unlike any other.

Education Ages 18+ Cultural exchange Language immersion Quality · Ethics · Sharing
Giving meaning to life

A humanitarian mission with real impact

Madagascar lacks infrastructure but is rich in its land, its people and its landscapes. Carrying out a project here — and seeing its impact on children with your own eyes — is a trigger to give more meaning to your life.

No specific degree or experience is required. What matters is preparation: months of exchange with our on-site teams, a good grasp of the volunteer guide, and an open heart. Volunteering is about sharing — everyone gives and receives in turn before leaving the island.

Save the planet

Unlock languages

The best age to learn a language is before six. Native-speaking volunteers open doors monolingual children would never otherwise have.

Champion children's rights

The programme promotes the Convention on the Rights of the Child and the elimination of discrimination against women.

Rice culture, zebu, famadihana feasts, music, dance, crafts and gastronomy — a multicultural society to discover without hesitation.
