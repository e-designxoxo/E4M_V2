Who we are · Governance

Transparent governance.
Four countries, one framework.

Education for Madagascar operates under four legally distinct entities — in Madagascar, France, the United Kingdom and Switzerland — each governed by elected boards and bound by shared statutes and values.

Legal registrations

Four legal entities, one mission

Each entity operates under the laws of its country, governed by its elected board, contributing to the same field programmes in Madagascar.
