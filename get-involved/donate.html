Seven years of action

Breaking the
cycle.

In Madagascar, investing in education from an early age is crucial to breaking the cycle of poverty — providing children with a solid foundation to contribute to an educated and inclusive society.

In 2023, Education For Madagascar created the AKKA Montessori School to make a greater impact — starting to care for children from an early age, all from underprivileged families.

From Montessori classrooms to reforestation plots, from prenatal care to coding workshops — each of our 13 programmes addresses a different barrier that keeps a child from reaching their potential.

Education For Madagascar is a Charity in England, a non-profit in France and Switzerland, and an NGO in Madagascar.

Members are the backbone of Education for Madagascar. When you register, you become a formal part of the association — one of the people whose name is behind this work.

Annual membership is £20 for the UK entity. Equivalent memberships exist for France, Switzerland and Madagascar.

Membership is distinct from donation. Donating does not automatically make you a member.
